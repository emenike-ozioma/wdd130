Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help customers navigate rough waters by supplying them with the equipment and/or the guide they need to safely head down the river.

Audience

To anyone who is in good health from 8 years and above and who wants to try something new and exciting. To anyone that wants to have fun with a bunch of friends and who wants to experience nature from a different prospective. To outdoor sport lovers and to who wants to challenge challenge himself. To kids with their parent. To school groups and companies. To who wants to celebrate a bachelor/bachelorette party in an original and adventurous way. Anyone can access this site through a desktop, laptop, and a mobile device.
