Lagos, Nigeria

Lagos is a major economic centre of Nigeria. It would be the fifth largest economy in Africa if it were a country. It is Nigeria's largest city, built on four main islands - Lagos, Iddo, Ikoyi, and Victoria - that are connected to each other and to the mainland by bridges. Its population is centered on Lagos Island, on the Bight of Benin. It was the capital (1960-1991) of independent Nigeria untill Abuja became the new capital. It is a major trade and industrial centre
